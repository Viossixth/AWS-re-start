## Overview

Nastro Bliss is a beloved restaurant situated at Johannesburg known for contemporary African cuisine and specializing in grill and fire menu items. Catering to local residence, corporate partners and to tourists to showcase their take and expertise in on African cuisine. The restaurant is ready to scale operations and enhance how customers connect with the business.
