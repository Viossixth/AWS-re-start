## Content Delivery Services


AWS offers a Global Accelerator as a means to imrpove availability and speed of customer applications. This is done by directing traffic throught eh global AWS netowrk. Two types exist a standard accelerator and a custom accelerator which can be customised by the customer to suit their business and organizational needs.
Global Accelerators imprve network performance , protects applications from DDOS Attacks and deliver highly available applications. AWS also offers a Content Delivery Network through CloudFront. Cloudfront stores informatiom frequently accessed by users closest to them to avoid slow loading apps. CDN's focus mostly on caching, dynamic acceleration and edge locations computations.

Amazon also offers Elastic Load Balancer aiming to distribute the incoming traffic to other instances to avoid a scenario where servers are overwhelmed. This improves application performance,performs health checks ,easily add or remove servers and can handle encryption and decryption.
Elastic Load Balancer has four types of load balancers namely  an Application Load Balancer, Network Load Balancer, Gateway Load Balancer and Classic Load Balancer.

Amazon Route 53 is AWS Domain Name Service, translating URL's into IP Addresses. Route 53 is used for domain registration, DNS routing and health checks. It is highly available and scalable service which intergrates easily with other aws services.
Route 53 has four routing policies namely Simple routing, Failover routing  , Latency routing and Weighted routing policy
